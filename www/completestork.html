Complete Stork Bundle

(twenty-five dollars per hour)



To provide Mom with the best care, we follow the "rest and care" plan we establish in our planning session.

As a new mother, you have plenty to do. Let me take care of any correspondences you need to keep up with such as emails, letters, thank you cards, etc.

After a new baby, many will want to send their well wishes to the family. Allow me to assist in coordinating time with well-wishers along with any professional correspondence.

Let me care for your bundle as you take a needed rest. Your child is in excellent hands. From supporting breast or bottle feedings, to batheing, dressing, and diaper changes. I have it covered.

If you have other lovely children that need looking after, I am able to care for them by providing general child care supervision.

With a new addition to your family, grocery shopping and meal preparation are often the last thing on your mind. Please let me attend to these for you, observing any dietary considerations.

I want you to enjoy your new baby and get well-deserved rest. Leave general "tidying and picking up after" to me. Allow me to make the beds and pick up the toys.

Within the allotted time, I will handle not only light chores, but general housekeeping as well. This includes laundry, vacuuming, sweeping, dusting in family areas and emptying the indoor trash.

I will assist you in attending the many necessary doctor appointments.
Additionally, any errands which need be performed outside the house, within ten miles, I will gladly attend to.

**Each bundle requires an initial three-hour minimum. Beyond that, a minimum of two hours is required for each subsequent visit.
