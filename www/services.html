Services

Friends of the Stork Can Help If You:

  • are expecting
  • have just adopted a baby
  • are adjusting to life with a newborn
  • just got home from the hospital
  • are a first time mom
  • have other small children who need care
  • are on mandated bed rest or other restrictions
  • family is not able to provide all the help and support

  • had a Caesarian section
  • partner/husband is not available to do everything

  • Thoughtful assessment of your unique situation
  • Careful planning to help you rest
  • Positive emotional support
  • Polite management for well-wishers
  • Skillful infant care
  • Unbiased support for breast and/or bottle feeding
